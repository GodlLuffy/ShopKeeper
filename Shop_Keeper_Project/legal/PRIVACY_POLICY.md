# Privacy Policy for ShopKeeper

**Effective Date: March 30, 2026**

Welcome to **ShopKeeper**. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our application.

## 1. Information We Collect
We collect information that you directly provide to us, including:
- **Registration Details**: Name, email address, password, and shop name.
- **Business Data**: Inventory items, sales records, and transactions (stored securely via Google Firebase).
- **Device Information**: Model, operating system, and unique device identifiers for security purposes.

## 2. How We Use Information
We use the collected information to:
- Provide and maintain the ShopKeeper application.
- Secure your account via 4-digit PIN and biometrics.
- Synchronize your business data across devices using Google Cloud services.
- Communicate with you regarding account updates and verification emails.

## 3. Data Storage & Security
We use **Google Firebase** and **Firestore** for real-time cloud synchronization. Your data is encrypted in transit and at rest using industry-standard security protocols. 

## 4. Third-Party Services
Our application uses:
- **Google OAuth**: To facilitate secure sign-in and account verification.
- **Google Firebase**: For cloud database and authentication services.

## 5. Your Choices
You may access, update, or delete your account information at any time via the profile settings within the app.
